The BIG why

Currently in the Stockholm region, the only way to know when your age group can start to get vaccinated is check the news or receive a physical letter from 1177.se which states that a new vaccination group can book a vaccination appointment. Keep reading below.

This slooooow way to be aware when you can start making an appointment is what our mailing list wants to solve. By signing up for our mail list you will know at once when 1177.se have opened bookings for a new vaccination group (i.e when the boxes in the image above where it says “Ännu inte öppen” opens) on their vaccination information webpage. Instead of waiting for lagging news or letters, you can be quick to find out when you can make a vaccination appointment.

Follow these steps to make it possible:

1. Sign up for our mailing list in the form below

We will never share your email with anyone else and we will delete all stored emails when all vaccination times are available on 1177. We do not take any responsibility for your vaccination booking.
The reason for creating this web application is that we currently believe Folkhälsomyndigheten is very vague regarding when and how specific age groups in Sweden can begin to book their vaccination appointment.
